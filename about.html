History of school

    RPBS ZPHS SRIKALAHASTI(BOYS) was established in 1902 and it is managed by the Local Body. It is located in urban area . It is located in SRIKALAHASTI block of CHITTOOR district of ANDHRA PRADESH. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 10. The school is co-educational and it does not have an attached pre-primary section. The school is non-residential in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. During the previous academic year; the school functioned for 221 days.

Academics in School

   

This school consist of 6th to 10th classes.It is co-educational school.It doesn't have primary school.Telugu and English are the medium of instructions in this school.
